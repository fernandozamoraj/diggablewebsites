SERVICES

Providing website design needs for the underdog. My designs are simple and intended to provide a solution for small business (aka the mom and pop shop).

All websites are created to be light weight and responsive to allow easy viewing in any computer or mobile device.


PORTFOLIO

Graduated from Tarleton State University with a B.S. in Computer Science in 2001 after 8 years of of honorable service with the U.S. army. After that I graduated from Texas A&M University of Central Texas with a Masters in Information Systems.

I've been a professional computer programmer for approximately fourteen years. Designing websites allows me to do the fun part of web development.

Currently I have a day time job which occupies my regular programming duties. I can only do this because I approach with the simple design process in mind. Many business advertise the no-job-is-to-big. My approach is different and that is if that if your website requires more than a weekend worth of work, than the project is too large for me.
