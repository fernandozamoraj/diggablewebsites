THE SIMPLE WEBSITE CONCEPT

Creating a website is a challenge. Even with all the advances in do-it-your-self tools, website creation can still be a challenge.

No matter how simple developers try to make the tools, they simply push the complexity elsewhere. Elsewhere is usually in the learning curve associated with the tool.

That's why we recommend that you leave the development to the experts. That way you can focus on the things that matter like running your business.

The simple website concept is intended to give you a website presence at a low price. If your website needs require e-commerce then a simple website may not be a viable solution for you.

WHY DIGGABLE WEBSITES

Our process provides the quality and professionalism, all at an affordable price!

My philosophy is based on providing the customer what they need. The simple process is not for everyone. The web is filled with plenty of do-it-yourself tools. Just about anyone can be build a website. At least that's the pitch. The reality is that even it's simplest form, publishing a professional website can still be quite a challenge. This challenge can take you many hours. Those hours can end up spread over several weeks or months of time. That's time that you can be dedicating towards the operation of your business.

That's were Diggable Websites comes in. We take away all of the hassle of designing and publishing a website out of your hands and reduce to a few simple steps. Steps that do not even require you to touch a computer. Well you will have to touch a computer but only to view and confirm your site. Other than that, the only thing that you have to do is describe your business, consult us with your idea and we provide the solution. We provide access to a temporary site over the internet so that you can see the design and provide feedback. You can view the website right from your mobile device. We cut out the hours of research and the headache associated with registering the domain, uploading files to the host, administration and so on and so on.

From there forward you pay a yearly fee to continue hosting the site. If you should ever decide that you would like someone else to maintain the registry of your website we would allow you to transfer your domain. We would also provide with all of the source code so that you can take the website and do what you wish with it.

MY SERVICES

Providing website design needs for the underdog. My designs are simple and intended to provide a solution for small business (aka the mom and pop shop).

All websites are created to be light weight and responsive to allow easy viewing in any computer or mobile device.

Designs that are viewable in all form factors

Our designs are made with the mobile layout in mind upfront. Many do-it-yourself designs do not implement a mobile design up front. As a result the website is viewable only on a desktop system. These days it is more probable that your customers will view your website on their mobile device, smart phone, iPad, or other tablet. Diggable Websites is aware of this fact and we start our design with that concept upfront. When that concept is not started upfront, it usually takes an entire re-write of your website. That could mean extra expenses and time.
